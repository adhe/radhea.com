`/images/lucidity-featured.jpg`

Lucidity Insights is a UAE-based platform built for startup founders and ecosystem builders across the Middle East and Africa. The platform aggregates intelligence, investor data, and founder tools into a single destination — positioning itself as the go-to hub for the MEA startup economy.

The project covered end-to-end brand design and full website design. The challenge was to establish a visual identity that felt credible and modern in the competitive startup media space — while remaining approachable to founders at any stage. The brand needed to work across web, newsletter, social media, and data-heavy content formats.

The design direction draws from the clarity and authority of financial and tech media, while staying accessible and regionally relevant. A clean typographic system paired with a structured layout gives the platform a sense of trustworthiness, while the brand's mascot and motion elements add personality.

`/images/lucidity-detail.jpg`

## Result

The platform launched successfully and is now live at lucidityinsights.com, serving startup founders and investors across the MEA region. The design system scaled across multiple content types — from long-form reports and podcasts to a startup database and investor directory — maintaining visual consistency throughout.

Additional: Since the approved design mockup was developed and the development keeps growing until now it has more than 10.000 visitors and generate revenue.
